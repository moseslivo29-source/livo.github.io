SMART REPORTING FOR TANZANIAN SCHOOLS

School Issue Reporting & Tracking System (SIRTS)

SIRTS is a simple digital platform that allows students and teachers to report school problems quickly and follow their progress until they are resolved. It replaces slow verbal reporting with a clear, trackable system.

The system focuses on real challenges such as broken infrastructure, shortage of learning materials, discipline issues, and teacher lateness.

Report An Issue View Status

Key Features

  • Digital issue reporting by students and teachers
  • Categories: infrastructure, discipline, academics, resources
  • Status tracking: new, pending, resolved
  • Simple dashboard for school management

About The Project

This project is designed for Tanzanian schools that still depend on informal or verbal reporting of problems. Many issues are delayed, forgotten, or never reach school leadership. SIRTS aims to make reporting more organised, transparent, and accountable.

How SIRTS Works

1. Report: A student or teacher fills a simple digital form describing the issue.

2. Categorise: The issue is placed under a clear category (e.g. infrastructure, discipline).

3. Assign: School management sees the report and assigns it to a responsible person.

4. Track: The status moves from new to pending to resolved, with a visible history.
